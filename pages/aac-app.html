What is the AAC App?

The AAC App is designed for individuals who are non-verbal or have speech impairments. It allows users to communicate using symbol-based buttons that generate speech.

Key Features

  • Text-to-Speech with icon-based interface
  • Customizable categories and symbols
  • Offline access
  • User-friendly for all ages
